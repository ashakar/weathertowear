How to Use

How it works

On the homepage, you will be provided a brief summary of the current outdoor conditions as well as some recommendations on what to wear outside. The clothing recommendations are directly from what you have indicated as your clothing preferences. If you have already specified your target location, that will be used; otherwise, the weather at your device's location will be referenced. Clothing options, location, along with the various ranges used for temperature interperations can be changed anytime on the customize page.


A more thorough report of the current weather can be accessed through the forecast page, located near the bottom of the home page.

Account settings

Change your username and password on the account page.

Note: You will be asked to provide your current password before making any changes to your account

Logging out

Done using the app? Click the logout button. Any changes made to preferences or your account will be saved.
